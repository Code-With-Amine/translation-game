ABOUT THE GAME

The Game gives you the chance to choose two languages the first one is what the source language is, and the second one is the targeted language, After you Entered your choices you will start the game you have up to 1 minute to guess the right translation, If it passed and you haven't submitted your answer you will lose and the right answer will be shown to you ,furthermore, you will see a hint that show you how close you are to the word. When you've done with the game you can simply click the result button to see how many questions you got right if you want to restart the game you can simply press the restart button.
